Sunday Morning!

P Pooja Borse · Feb 11, 2024 · 2 min read

At 6:30 in the morning, I turned off the alarm. I freshened up and began my daily morning habits and exercise routine. Even though it was 7:30 in the morning today, there was no ray of sunlight, and the weather encouraged me to skip exercise. Instead of working out, I adjusted myself into a comfortable position, played my favorite song on low volume, and took some pictures of the sky and myself. The light was so lovely that I couldn't resist capturing the moment. That's how my Sunday started — lazy, beautiful, and fresh.

I don't know how, but I find myself more attracted to the moon, stars, sun, and the universe than anything else.

"if you ever feel low, consider yourself a star with self-light."

You can turn it ON and OFF whenever you want; just don't keep it ON for others. If you feel tired, switch OFF the light and save your energy!

Sometimes, I wonder whether I should relax or work more in the future. It's always a competition. Why do I study hard? If I do, then I should enjoy it, but different goals are awaiting you in the future. So, should you enjoy the current phase or keep working for the next part? My brain responds on both sides, and that's how confused it is.

Many such questions are perplexing, and you just can't find answers for them. You just can't. When you discuss something like this with others, after a lot of discussion, you may end up with an answer similar to "after all, in the end, it happens as written in destiny," and then you come back to the universe and the stars.

You know, when you keep coming back to the same thing, it's not less than love. That's how romantic the universe, stars, galaxies, and every tiny thing in the universe are — and so are you!

Happy Valentine's Week ❤ — Pooja
