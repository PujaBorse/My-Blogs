an essay on family

Under the Same Sky

P Pooja Borse · Feb 8, 2023 · 2 min read

Everyone in their late twenties must have similar childhood memories. Some of you may recall that everyone was sleeping together in Nani's or Dadi's house, playing for the entire day with card games, Ludo, picnics, business games, video games, and so on.

From those days to the present, everyone must have gone through an emotional rollercoaster ride as they grew up — some of them moving for higher education, some getting married and moving to different cities, and some moving abroad, only returning once a year. Everyone grew up along the way.

Thanks to technology, we are always connected to one another via social media. In this fast-paced world, a small thing happened a few days ago that put a big smile on my face.

I went outside to get some groceries around 8 p.m. and got stuck at one signal. I was waiting for the signal to open when the full moon in the sky caught my eye. After some time, the signal is activated, and I proceed home with my groceries.

After dinner, Bunny and I went for a walk on the terrace. We can see moonlight coming up the stairs as we walk up them. When we arrived at the terrace, Bunny pointed out that it was a full moon, and even stars could be seen. We're both enjoying our moonlight walk.

In between rounds, my phone's notification rings. I checked messages and found one from a brother who is currently abroad, sharing a photo of the full moon and simply saying that even stars are visible. It made me laugh out loud.

"even if everyone is not in the same place, all are under the same sky."
same moon, different windows. — Pooja
