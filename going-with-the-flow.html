an essay on wandering

Going with the Flow!

P Pooja Borse · Oct 8, 2023 · 3 min read

Have you ever stopped and wondered what makes you tick, what makes you uniquely you? You see, personality is this crazy blend of characteristics and style. Me? I'm what you'd call a "go-with-the-flow" kind of person. It might not be the usual way to describe things, but it's basically me saying I'm all about change, rolling with whatever comes my way, and just enjoying the wild ride of life.

I'm quite an enthusiast when it comes to diving into stuff about personalities and how people operate. You know what I've realized? I lean towards having a dynamic personality. What does that mean? Basically, I'm not one to get stuck in the same zone, place, mood, or routine. I thrive on the ever-changing ride of life, embracing both personal growth and the transformation of my surroundings.

Back in school, my dad had this idea that really stuck with me. He motivated me to study diligently, all while dreaming of a future where I'd be working in different spots and enjoying breakfast, lunch, and dinner in all sorts of places, each with its own tasty food culture. That idea has never left my mind. It's not just about adventure; it's also a celebration of the awesomeness of diversity.

As I scribble down these thoughts on a laid-back Sunday evening, I'm on a flight to Pune. That evening sky with all its colorful shades has really captured my heart. It's like a nudge about the amazing magic that nature and its simple beauty bring.

Sometimes life hands us the dreams we've been wishing for, and it's a real head-scratcher. Do we dive right in, or do we hold back, worried that there might be a catch? It's a question that keeps us up at night, especially when we've learned the hard way that gaining something often means losing something else. When I'm in those reflective moments, I usually chill out and let life do its thing. I figure its mysteries will reveal themselves when the time is right.

"our love for a city isn't always about perfect weather."

You know, even though we're all about seeking new adventures, there's this deep-down truth that hits us right in the feels. The coziness of home? Yeah, that often comes alive in the places where we spent our college years or even back in the day during school. It's usually about the vibes we've got going on, the feelings we've wrapped around a place that makes us see its weather through rose-coloured glasses.

Beyond personal preferences, my love for travel and exploration knows no bounds. I am drawn to new places, diverse cities, and the opportunity to immerse myself in various cultures. These experiences broaden my horizons, enrich my understanding of the world, and ultimately shape me into the person I am meant to become.

Embracing a dynamic personality — one that thrives on change and growth — opens the door to a world teeming with opportunities and experiences. Life's beauty lies in its perpetual state of transformation. Whether you're someone who seeks the comfort of routine or, like me, relishes the thrill of change, remember that life's true essence lies in its ever-evolving nature. Through travel, exploration, and the embrace of diverse cultures, we not only discover the world but also uncover the layers of our own selves, evolving into the individuals we are destined to become.

still figuring it out, one flight at a time. — Pooja
