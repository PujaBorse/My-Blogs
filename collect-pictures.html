an essay on people

Collect Pictures

P Pooja Borse · Jul 2, 2023 · 2 min read

This world is full of stories. Some you watch in the movies, some in real life, and in some cases, you heard it. The more people you connect with, the more stories you have with you. The best part about it is, you can take what you like in the story and leave the rest. Every part you take adds colour to your life, and that is how you paint your story.

I always have this from my childhood, or I must say when I start understanding the outer world. The bond where you respect, cherish, and welcome new changes always gives you more power. It doesn't matter what is coming to you; what matters is what you are taking from it. It doesn't matter if it is long years of friendship or just the first meet. Also, it doesn't matter if you are meeting same-age people or younger or older. You feel the power when you are with good people.

"you are always busy collecting pictures — not photos, but the pictures you're living."

You are going to the office, you are preparing your favourite food, you are meeting your old friends, you are meeting new people. You are driving your car, you are meeting your love, you are laughing with your loved one, you are preparing for your exams, you are buying your house, you are waiting for your friends, and many more.

Be busy living pictures in real life — make sure you have all the collection you wanted.

still collecting, one ordinary day at a time. — Pooja
