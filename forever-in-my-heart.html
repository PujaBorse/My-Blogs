Forever in My Heart

P Pooja Borse · 1 min read

Sometimes, adulthood proves challenging. Even indulging in midnight ice cream fails to alleviate the difficulty; acidity intensifies, and completing tasks brings no satisfaction.

On a day when you manage responsibilities, enjoy a hearty meal, and watch your favorite movie, you'll still reminisce about moments with your favorite person. The poignant truth is that despite wealth, health, education, knowledge, and sensibility, you can't create new moments with someone no longer in this world. Thousands of memories will replay in your mind, leading to tearful realizations that time holds more power than any possessions or capabilities.

"time holds more power than any possessions or capabilities."

It's a profound understanding that words may struggle to convey. Humility becomes essential, acknowledging the uncertainty of what lies ahead.
