an evening, unhurried

Roaming

P Pooja Borse · Oct 13, 2022 · 2 min read

It is 5 o'clock in the evening. I put the laptop aside and get out of the room to go to the terrace. The wind is playing at its best, clouds are running slower than usual, and birds are returning home. There are two shades in the sky differentiating the two parts of the sky. It is scenic. It is beautiful. It is a sunset.

How can a sunset be such a beautiful thing to experience always? Always, it is that soothing. I decided to take some rounds on the terrace to feel the excitement at that moment. Honestly, sometimes to feel the vibe you don't need the person — you can vibe with the sky as well. I usually vibe with the sky always, and it never lets me down.

"sometimes to feel the vibe, you don't need the person — you can vibe with the sky."

People are running, I can see that from the terrace, the main road going to the city is in front of my house. I am not sure if anyone has the same feeling or not, but I feel good when there are people around. Like I don't know them, maybe they are complete strangers to me, but it feels good when people are around. Of course, that does not mean I like to go in a crowd; if you know, then you know.

It was dark by then. I walk down to check if I have any updates or not, and to my goodness, nothing is there. As it is almost my log-off time, I shut all the tools and close the laptop. As I am alone at home, I decided to roam around the city with Reva. By the time I get ready, I asked her to come to Khushnuma café. I locked the home and rode my bike in the same direction.
